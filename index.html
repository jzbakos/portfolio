Introduction

This project was a great way for me to improve and showcase my knowledge on database design and web development/design. Along with those, communication and collaboration were both extremely important over the course of this project’s development. I was lucky enough to work with two students (Jack Ingram and Jarod Colley) that I have worked with before, which helped a lot in terms of chemistry.

We chose to work together as much as possible to ensure equal distribution of responsibility and workload. To do this, once or twice a week since the beginning of the semester we would get into a Discord call for a few hours and work through whatever we had to. This worked very well for us, we were always either on-track or ahead of schedule.

I would say that we had a relatively balanced workload and each member of the team was necessary and useful throughout the duration of this project.

Communication

Every member of the team had excellent communication throughout the duration of the project. We created an all-purpose Discord group chat to use as our primary communication medium. Anytime a message was sent in the chat, one of the members would usually respond in less than 30 minutes.

There were never any issues with communication from anyone. I made sure to respond to any message ASAP. We also set meetings once or twice per week where we would get into a Discord call for an hour or two to coordinate. Then we would just do our tasks and update each other next meeting.

We had flawless communication throughout this project. I do think the communication was better due to the online format. Everyone is always home and near a device.

My Contribution

I’m confident that I contributed a sufficient amount to this project - if anything, I most likely contributed more than needed. The website development and design is my most significant contribution. I also assisted with writing the project report document, however, I would say I contributed more to the website than the report.

Application Development & Design

Database Design & Report

I would consider the quantity and quality of my contributions to both be very high, especially when talking about the application design and functionality. I created the website structure, hosted the application and database online, and designed most of the website.

To be clear: I chose to work many extra hours on designs that were not needed. I plan to use this website as a portfolio piece in the future and wanted to improve it every chance I could. There were a few nights I found myself up until 2 AM after falling into the rabbit hole of design improvements. Jack and Jarod were both very helpful when implementing small-medium functionality on the website too. Also, Jack was mainly in charge of the video editing and creation, which he did an amazing job on.

Take a look at the commit frequency on the GitHub Repository

Reflection

Looking back, I wouldn’t say that I learned anything new in regards to collaboration, but I definitely did improve in a lot of areas. As a new-ish developer/student I was very pleased to see all the higher level database design concepts come into play when structuring the application. As soon as the report and database design aspects were finished, it was just a matter of putting the pieces together. It’s nice to see years of theory turn into practice and I am excited to further explore the benefits of database design concepts.

Overall, I feel that each member of this group, myself included, contributed and collaborated wonderfully. I learned a lot along the way and am very proud of the final product.
